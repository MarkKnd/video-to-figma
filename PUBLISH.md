# Figma Community listing — draft copy

**Name**
Video → Figma Screenshots

**Tagline** (short, shown under the name)
Turn any video link into a clean filmstrip of frames on your canvas.

**Description**
Paste a video link (X, YouTube, Vimeo, and more) and drop screenshots straight
into Figma. The plugin detects scene cuts and suggests a frame plan, lets you
switch to fixed-interval capture, pick the quality, and choose the page to
export to. Frames land as a horizontal auto-layout stack, each sized to the
video's native aspect ratio — perfect for storyboards, UX teardowns, motion
references, and inspiration boards.

- Scene-cut detection or every-N-seconds capture
- Frames sized to the source aspect (16:9, 9:16, etc.)
- One clean auto-layout stack per export
- Flags earlier exports / duplicates already on the page
